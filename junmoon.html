Jun Moon

  • I am currently an Assitant Professor at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Seoul, South Korea. I was with the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST) as an Assitant Professor from Nov. 2015 to Feb. 2019. I received my Ph.D. degree (Dec. 2015) in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of Illinois, Urbana and Champaign (UIUC), USA, under the supervision of Professor Tamer Basar. I completed my M.S. (2008) and B.S. (2006) in Electrical Engineering and Electrical and Computer Engineering, respectively, all from Hanyang University, Seoul, South Korea. I was a researcher at Agency for Defense Development (ADD), South Korea, from 2008 to 2011. I am a recipient of the Fulbright Graduate Study Award 2011.
